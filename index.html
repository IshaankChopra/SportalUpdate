Idea Implementation

We will develop an IoT app that harnesses different IoT technologies to improve the efficiency of the current booking system.

  • This revolutionary mobile application will be the center of all IoT technologies implemented.
  • The app will deliver any service a customer or an administration requires, based on the latest technological advancements made.
  • Through the use of a fully automated booking system, our vision is to minimize the involvement of humans as far as possible.

  • Booking made easy

    A customer can formerly book for themselves and for others by paying online. Booking details will be emailed to them and added to their calendar (if they give permission).

  • Cancellations made easy

    Should the customer choose to cancel the booking, the cancellation fee will be calculated based on factors including the rate at which the facilities were booked, the rate of cancellation, time remaining until the activity, weather

  • Weather update while booking

    Potential bad weather will be notified to the user through the applicatio

  • Time Up reminder

    In case the customer fails to leave the premises within the time allowed, he or she will be notified verbally and via phone notifications

Additional Features

Weather Details

An open source weather API will be used inform the customer about the predicted weather at the venue for the date and time the customer is looking to book for. If it’s predicted to rain, then the venue will be temporarily closed for booking at that moment unless the prediction to rain is averted in the future by the weather forecast.

Enter with ease

At the sports arena, an automated two-way turnstile gate will be installed for entry and exit, granting only one person to enter and exit at a time. On either side of the gates, a tiny Bluetooth beacon will be equipped. In the “My Booking” menu, a new booking is created whenever a person makes a booking for an arena. This booking will be assigned a unique ID in the database. Getting the smartphone with that booking close to the gate would allow the person to verify his booking. The gate will then verify the booking in the database via IOT and open once it finds the unique booking ID.

Leave Reminder

In case the customer fails to leave the premises within the time allowed, he or she will be notified verbally and via phone notifications

IoT

Pricing

Pricing will vary for each sports facility. Check the Terms and Conditions.

Government Sector

  • We will be offering our software side (admin login) to be used by the Government in the form of a royalty contract deal. We will be earning 10% per booking fee and 20% per cancellation fee throughout the term of the deal.
  • The Government will initially hire us and we will be installing the hardware such as the custom turnstile gates with the Bluetooth beacon technology implemented via IoT at the sports facilities, all covered by their own costs.
  • As the hardware and software would need to be checked and debugged, we will also charge a monthly maintenance fee.
